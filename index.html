Cover Letter for Solita Data Academy 2022
30th June 2022

Dear Solitans,

I’ve been eyeing on this opportunity since I graduated, and now it was my time to apply for the Solita Data Academy. Here comes a bit about my background. In the latter part of my studies (Erasmus exchange) and after my graduation, life took me to another direction, and I’ve been living abroad for a few years now. I was always interested in my own field of studies, namely energy engineering, but I did not find a satisfying enough job here in Poland, so I started working as a freelance translator! The freedom of freelancer life is great and I’ve been enjoying it by traveling as much as I could during these few not so easy years for crossing borders. Now I’m ready to move back to Finland in the search for something more technical and challenging.

Since my first engineering summer job I’ve been always working with data. Mostly the tool in use was Excel, and I know that still there are a lot of industries and energy companies in Finland relying on Excel for data analysis and even data management. It is undeniably a great universal tool, but already about five years back I saw the problem with Excel reports in different servers getting messed up when different people were using or working on them. I always had a hunger to learn and use better tools for data management and visualization, but yet I haven’t had the opportunity to do it professionally. During the last couple of years, I’ve been lightly studying about Pandas Dataframes and similar subjects. I feel that anything more complicated is hard to learn without real world data and a real need behind it.

In my previous jobs and studies, I always enjoyed making different visualizations and even built a couple of dashboards for real-life use, of course again in Excel. I feel like these ‘new’ tools like Power BI and Tableau are quite intuitive and rather similar to Excel pivot tables and graphs, thus I have some applicable experience for this path. On the other hand, during my studies I got very interested in programming, and acquired decent skills in Python and C++, so maybe we could find a mixture of the Data Engineer and Data Visualization Specialist paths most suitable for me.

As a person I’m relaxed and co-operative. Sometimes I might be a bit of a perfectionist, but I also know how to compromise. I always finish what I’ve promised and can be relied on. Solita Data Academy feels like a perfect occasion for me right now.

Looking forward to hearing from you,

Saku Jussila
